# Adlam's Frame: comment on \"Wigner's Frame\"

> **Source:** Originally published on [arXiv quant-ph](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.18383) on July 22, 2026.

In a recent work, Adlam argues that this tension can be dissolved by appealing to quantum reference frames (QRFs). We present and critically assess her proposal.
